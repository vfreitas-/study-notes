## Greatest Common Divisor (GCDs)

*Definition*: For integers *a* and *b*, their greatest common divisor or gcd(a,b) is the largest integer *d* so that *d* divides both *a* and *b*.

*Input*: Integers *a*,*b* >= 0
*Output*: gcd(a,b)
